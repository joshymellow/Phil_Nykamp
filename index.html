Your go-to podcast for all things real estate and community in West Michigan! ABOUT PHIL NYKAMP

Phil Nykamp is a real estate agent with Keller Williams Harbortown, helping people confidently buy and sell homes throughout West Michigan. With deep local roots, 28 years of customer service experience, and a passion for community, Phil brings a wealth of knowledge on local neighborhoods, current market conditions, and what it takes to make the real estate experience exceptional.

On The Keys to the Community, Phil combines his real estate expertise with his love for connecting people. Each episode features interviews with local business owners, community leaders, and industry professionals, offering insight into the real estate market and the vibrant life in West Michigan’s neighborhoods. The Keys to the Community is more than just about buying and selling, it’s about building relationships and loving where you live. Tune in for inspiring stories, real estate tips, and local happenings that make the community thrive.
