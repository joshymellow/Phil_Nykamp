The Keys To The Community

Phil Nykamp

Your go-to podcast for all things real estate and community in West Michigan! Hosted by Phil Nykamp, a Keller Williams Harbortown agent with 28 years of customer service experience and deep local roots, The Keys to the Community focuses on real estate education and connecting with the people who make the community special.

Each episode features interviews with local business owners, community leaders, and industry professionals, offering insight into the real estate market and the vibrant life in West Michigan’s neighborhoods.

Whether you’re a first-time homebuyer, a seller looking to navigate the market, or simply interested in the happenings in your community, this podcast is the perfect blend of real estate education and community connection.
