The Keys To The Community

Hosted by Phil Nykamp, a Keller Williams Harbortown agent with 28 years of customer service experience and deep local roots, The Keys to the Community is your go-to podcast for all things real estate and community in West Michigan.

Each episode features interviews with local business owners, community leaders, and industry professionals, offering insight into the real estate market and the vibrant life in West Michigan's neighborhoods. From the historic snowmelt system in downtown Holland to the people building businesses that make this region special—Phil brings you the stories that matter.

Whether you're a first-time homebuyer, a seller looking to navigate the market, or simply interested in the happenings in your community, this podcast is the perfect blend of real estate education and community connection.
